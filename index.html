Myths vs Facts

Myth: HIV can spread through touch or saliva
Fact: HIV spreads only through specific body fluids

HIV Prevention

Protect yourself and others

Safe Practices

  • Always use condoms correctly
  • Never share needles
  • Get tested with new partners

SMS "PREVENT" to 33445 for free condom locations

PrEP (Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis)

Daily medication that reduces HIV risk by 99%

Free PrEP Clinics: SMS "PREP [Your Area]" to 55667

Mother-to-Child Prevention

With proper treatment, risk can be reduced to <1%

Pregnant? SMS "PMTCT" to 77889 for prenatal care

HIV Treatment

Live healthy with proper care

ARV Therapy

  • Free antiretroviral (ARV) medicines available
  • Take medications as prescribed
  • Viral load can become undetectable

ARV Locations: SMS "ARV [Your Town]" to 11223

Medication Adherence

Remember Your Meds:

  • Set phone alarms
  • Use pill organizers
  • SMS "REMIND" to 44556 for daily reminders

Nutrition & Wellness

Healthy Living Tips:

Testing FAQs

Q: How soon after exposure should I test?
A: Wait 3 months for conclusive results

Q: Are tests confidential?
A: Yes, by law your status is private
